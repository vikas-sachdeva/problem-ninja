Given a number n, find the highest power of 2 that divides n.

**Example-1**:

**Input**: 

    n = 48

**Output**: 

    16

**Explanation**: Highest power of 2 that divides 48 is 16. 

**Example-2**:

**Input**: 

    n = 5
    
**Output**: 

    1
    
**Explanation**: Highest power of 2 that divides 5 is 1.
